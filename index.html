About Me

Who am I?

I am a recent grad who is interested in Data Science and wants to use technology and data to make the world a better place. While I love physics, I have learned in my research that solving puzzles in data science is incredibly rewarding and powerful. I hope to continue working in the data science field in a way that I can make people's lives better.

What is my background?

I have a bachelor's degree in Phyiscs from UC Davis and have been doing research in physics for the past couple years. Currently I am working in cosmology studying galaxy cluster mergers and would love to tell you more about it. Drop me a line or check out my group's publication on the work I've been doing further down the page.

What work experience do I have?

I financed my degree with many jobs dating back to high school. I've worked at a ski resort, grocery store, cafeteria, library, and physics laboratory in the past few years. From these places I've learned how to work in a team, lead projects, and have fun while getting a job done!

What do I do for fun?

I love hiking, running, reading, and anything that involves being outside and with people. I hope to travel all around the world and meet new, exciting people and experience different cultures.
